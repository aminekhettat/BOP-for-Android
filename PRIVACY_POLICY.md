# Privacy Policy for Back-Office Player (BOP)

**Last Updated: May 15, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Back-Office Player ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. 

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
Back-Office Player is designed with a **Privacy-First** philosophy.
- **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (PII).
- **Offline Functionality:** The App operates primarily offline. Your audio files and practice data stay on your device.
- **Media Access:** The App requires permission to access audio files on your device solely to provide playback and looping functionality. This data is never uploaded to any server.

## 2. Data Storage
- **Settings & History:** Your practice history and app settings are stored locally on your device using Android's secure internal storage.
- **Third-Party Services:** The App uses the Google Play In-App Update API to check for updates. This process is governed by Google Play's own privacy policy.

## 3. Permissions
- **READ_MEDIA_AUDIO:** Used to load and play your audio files.
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE:** Used to maintain audio playback when you switch apps or lock your screen.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
